CrosshairX draws the reticle on top of your game windows. Make sure your game runs in "windowed fullscreen" or "borderless window" mode. This setting allows the overlay to appear correctly over the game content. If the crosshair hides behind the game, check your taskbar to ensure the CrosshairX window stays active in the background.

## 🚧 Common troubleshooting

*   Performance drops: Lower the refresh rate of the overlay in the settings menu if you experience lag.
